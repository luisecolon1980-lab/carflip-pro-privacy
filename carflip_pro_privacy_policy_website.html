Privacy Policy

CarFlip Pro

Last updated: [March 3, 2026]

1. Introduction

CarFlip Pro ("the Application") is a return on investment (ROI) calculator designed to help vehicle investors analyze potential profits when buying and reselling vehicles.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

2. Information We Collect

CarFlip Pro does not collect, store, or share personal information.

The Application:

  • Does not require user registration or account creation.
  • Does not request name, email address, or phone number.
  • Does not access location, camera, microphone, or contacts.
  • Does not use cookies.
  • Does not use advertisements.
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools.
  • Does not transmit data to external servers.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Any financial data entered by the user (such as purchase price, repair costs, resale value, and calculated ROI) is stored locally on the user's device solely for functional purposes.

  • Data remains on the user's device.
  • Data is not shared with third parties.
  • Data is deleted when the application is uninstalled.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect certain technical information according to its own privacy policies. CarFlip Pro does not control and is not responsible for data collection performed by the platform provider.

5. Security

Because the Application does not collect or transmit personal data, the risk of data exposure is minimal.

6. Children's Privacy

CarFlip Pro is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.
